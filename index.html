Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in School of Information Management at Wuhan University(WHU), a beautiful university with long history, where I am majored in Library and Information Science. At WHU, I count myself lucky to be one of the troublemakers in the Information Retrieval and Knowledge Mining Laboratory (irlab).

Academically, my interests lie in scientific text mining, scientometrics and informetrics (science of science), medical informatics & information retrieval. Methodologically, I am an opportunistic researcher. I do programming, statistics, data visualizations, bibliometrics, theory discussions in my studies.

I grew up in Tianmen, Hubei Province, a small city in central of China. I'm mad for reading novels. Always I enjoy reading books by kindle and weChat Reading. “Three Kingdoms”(《三国演义》), “Ordinary World”(《平凡的世界》)and "White Nightis"(《白夜行》) are my favorite three books. “The Girl with NO Name” is the first novel written in English I have read. Higashino Keigo(东野圭吾) is my favorite writer, I will finish all his novels someday! I love Jane Austen as well.

I love to running, playing badminton and playing tennis. My favorite athlete is Lin Dan, one of the greatest badminton players in the world. I am a relative active blogger (view blog here), where I write my mind and share my life. I am extremely enthusiastic about life, and in love with my study and am always on the hunt for learning something new.
